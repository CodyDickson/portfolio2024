Avoid the Squares (Game Demo)

UnityC#Game Dev
This is a game built in Unity that features top-down action with minimalistic graphics. The player moves with WASD and shoots bullets with the arrow keys. The objective of the game is to avoid the squares that spawn (they're the enemies) and earn enough points to activate the teleporter to move onto the next room. There are currently three rooms in this demo.

Enemies have a chance to drop a Power Up when defeated which provides a random benefit to the player such as increased damage, increased move speed, and bonus points. There is also a points Multiplier that increases each time 10 enemies are defeated without the player being hit, up to a Multiplier of 10. Getting hit once will reset the Multiplier back to its default of 1.
